Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji or Fugaku, located on the island of HonshÅ«, is the highest mountain in Japan, It is the second-highest volcano located on an island in Asia (after Mount Kerinci on the island of Sumatra), and seventh-highest peak of an island on Earth.

Covered with snow

Mount Fuji's exceptionally symmetrical cone, which is covered in snow for about five months of the year.

The mountain is located about 100 km (62 mi) southwest of Tokyo and is visible from there on clear days.

Cultural icon

It is commonly used as a cultural icon of Japan and it is frequently depicted in art and photography, as well as visited by sightseers and climbers.
